Number Within Hearts

When people chat online or by message, we always use popular internet slang, like “lol” - “laughing out loud” and “xoxo” - “kiss and hug you”. There is also a lot of internet slang around the world based on different cultural backgrounds.

In this project, I collected internet slang specifically relating to numbers, like “666” and “233”. What interested me is that when the same numerical internet slang is used in different countries, the meanings can be totally different or even opposite. For example, “666” represents “evil” in the west but means “awesome” in China. “555” means “laughing” in Thailand but “crying” in China. Wish this project could raise an interesting conversation between people with different cultural backgrounds and explore the story and meaning of the numbers.
